# ProvenanceDB Principles

## 1. Provenance First
Every fact should be traceable to its origin.

## 2. Trust as Data
Trust, confidence, verification, and review history are first-class data structures.

## 3. Explainability by Default
Knowledge should be explainable to both humans and AI systems.

## 4. Evidence Over Assertion
Claims should be supported by evidence whenever possible.

## 5. Human and Agent Collaboration
Humans and AI agents should operate on a shared, auditable knowledge foundation.

## 6. Open Standards
Schemas, protocols, and formats should remain open and interoperable.

## 7. Knowledge Has a Lifecycle
Knowledge changes. Systems should support review, expiration, and revalidation.

## 8. Transparency Over Opacity
Users should understand why information is trusted.
