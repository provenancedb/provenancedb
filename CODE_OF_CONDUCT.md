# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering an open, welcoming, and respectful community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful.
- Be constructive.
- Be professional.
- Assume good intentions.
- Focus on ideas rather than individuals.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment
- Personal attacks
- Discrimination
- Intimidation
- Deliberate disruption

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when necessary to maintain a healthy community.
